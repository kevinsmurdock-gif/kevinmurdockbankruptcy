The Timeline

What Actually Happened — In Order

A bankruptcy doesn't happen in a vacuum. Here is the documented, chronological record of what was built, what was achieved, and what federal prosecution did to it.

1992
The Citadel, Class of 1992 — Gold Star Graduate
Kevin S. Murdock graduated from The Citadel, The Military College of South Carolina. Gold Star recognition for academic achievement. Walk-on soccer. Early graduate. Shannon Forest Christian School before that.
2007
Founded Premier Medical Laboratory Services
Greenville, South Carolina. CLIA-certified clinical reference laboratory. The beginning of what would become a multi-state diagnostic network serving customers across all 50 US states.
March 24, 2020
First FDA-Validated COVID Lab in South Carolina
Premier Medical became the first private commercial laboratory in South Carolina validated for COVID-19 testing under FDA Emergency Use Authorization. Three weeks into the pandemic.
Yahoo Finance · MarketWatch · BioSpace · The Citadel (.edu)
2020
Free Testing for Every SC First Responder
Free COVID-19 testing for every law enforcement officer, firefighter, and EMT/paramedic in South Carolina. Thousands of N95 masks donated. Millions reinvested into expanding capacity and serving communities. Hired workers laid off from shuttered restaurants and businesses.
BioSpace · WYFF · MarketWatch · Yahoo Finance · Business Insider · UBJ
2020
Yale SalivaDirect Designated · 2M+ Specimens · 6M Kits
Designated by Yale University to run its SalivaDirect protocol. Over 2 million specimens processed. 6 million+ test kits manufactured locally through CPT Medical. Up to 300,000 tests per day with pooling. Blue Cross Blue Shield preferred lab in TX, SC, and NC. HHS surge testing sites in SC, AZ, UT, NC.
June 2021
US Senator Lindsey Graham Press Conference
Senator Graham held a televised press conference at the Premier Medical Laboratory in Greenville, SC. Kevin quoted: “As far as COVID goes, we are heavily invested in the variant testing and we're probably one of the largest labs in America that can do the volume.”
Newsweek · WSPA · WYFF · WJBF · WCBD · WSAV · Live5News
December 10, 2021
CDC Publishes MMWR Vol. 70, No. 49
The CDC’s Morbidity and Mortality Weekly Report documents the CDC deploying the identical pooled-testing methodology through the same eTrueNorth contractor pathway used by Premier Medical. Published at cdc.gov/mmwr.
CDC MMWR Vol. 70 No. 49 · December 10, 2021
February 2022
$51M Investment · 185 New SC Jobs · Governor Endorsed
$51 million capital investment creating 185 new jobs in Greenville County. Announced at SCBIO 2022, the state’s Life Sciences Conference. Published on the South Carolina Governor’s official .gov website. SC Secretary of Commerce endorsed on the record.
PRNewswire · SC Governor (.gov) · SCBIO 2022
2022
Six Labs Across Five States
Diversified Medical Healthcare operated six independently licensed, CLIA-certified laboratories: Premier Medical (Greenville SC), First Medical (Irving TX), Dynasty Medical (Hurricane UT), Meta Lab DX (Charlotte NC), Synapse Analytical (Raleigh NC), American Genetics Institute (Delray Beach FL). Each with its own lab director and state regulatory framework. All publicly announced via PRNewswire and SCBio.
PRNewswire · SCBio · 360Dx · BioUtah · divmedinc.com
2023–2024
Federal Prosecution Targets Premier Medical
Federal prosecution targeted one lab in a six-lab network. The methodology in question — pooled COVID-19 testing — is the same methodology the CDC itself deployed nationally through the same eTrueNorth contractor pathway, as documented in MMWR Vol. 70, No. 49. Five of the six DMH labs were never subject to enforcement action. Four of those five also ran COVID testing.
January 5, 2025
Chapter 11 Bankruptcy Filed
Kevin S. Murdock filed Chapter 11 bankruptcy — a direct consequence of the federal prosecution terminating the operating businesses that constituted Diversified Medical Healthcare. A company that had $51M invested, 450 employees, and a Governor’s endorsement 36 months earlier was forced to wind down because the prosecution made continued operation untenable.
The Context

What the Bankruptcy Coverage Omits

A bankruptcy filing is a public document. It tells you what someone owes. It does not tell you how they got there, what they built, or what was done to them. Here is the context.

$51M
The Business Was Viable
In February 2022 — 36 months before the bankruptcy filing — the SC Governor’s office endorsed a $51 million expansion creating 185 new jobs. The SC Secretary of Commerce went on the record. This was not a failing business. This was a business that was destroyed by prosecution.
1 of 6
One Lab Targeted in a Six-Lab Network
Diversified Medical Healthcare operated six independently licensed, CLIA-certified laboratories across five states. The prosecution focused on Premier Medical in Greenville, SC. The other five labs — including four that ran COVID testing — were never subject to enforcement action.
CDC
The CDC Used the Same Method
The CDC’s Morbidity and Mortality Weekly Report, Vol. 70, No. 49 (December 10, 2021), documents the CDC deploying the identical pooled-testing methodology through the same eTrueNorth contractor pathway. This is a federal public health record. It is not in dispute.
0
No Finding of Wrongdoing
Kevin’s attorney has stated on the public record that the Consent Judgment does not include any finding or admission of wrongdoing on Kevin’s part. The resolution was entered solely to avoid the cost, time, and uncertainty of a lengthy trial.
